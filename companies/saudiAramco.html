Saudi Aramco

Official Website

Saudi Aramco, officially known as the Saudi Arabian Oil Company, is a multinational petroleum and natural gas company based in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia. Established in 1933, it is one of the world's largest and most valuable energy companies. Saudi Aramco is state-owned and operates in all facets of the oil and gas industry, including exploration, production, refining, distribution, and marketing. It is renowned for its vast oil reserves and plays a significant role in the global energy market, supplying a substantial portion of the world's crude oil. The company's operations are crucial to the Saudi Arabian economy and have a substantial impact on the global energy landscape.

Saudi Aramco history

Saudi Aramco has a rich history that spans nearly a century. Here's a brief overview of its key milestones: 1933: Saudi Aramco is founded as a partnership between the Saudi Arabian government and the American oil company Standard Oil of California (SoCal). 1938: Oil is discovered in commercial quantities in the Dhahran region, leading to the establishment of the first commercial oil well in Saudi Arabia. 1950: Saudi Arabia acquires a 25% stake in Saudi Aramco, increasing its ownership in the company. 1973: Saudi Arabia, along with other oil-producing nations, institutes an oil embargo, leading to a significant increase in oil prices and increasing Saudi Aramco's importance in the global oil market. 1980s: Saudi Aramco takes full control of its operations and facilities, acquiring the remaining stake held by its American partners. 2016: Saudi Aramco unveils its financial data for the first time, indicating its status as one of the world's most profitable companies. 2019: Saudi Aramco goes public with an initial public offering (IPO), becoming the largest IPO in history at the time. Saudi Aramco's history is marked by its evolution from a small exploration venture to one of the largest and most influential energy companies globally, underpinning the Saudi Arabian economy and the global oil industry.
