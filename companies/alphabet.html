Alphabet Inc.

Official Website

Alphabet Inc. is a multinational conglomerate based in Mountain View, California, USA. It was established in 2015 as part of a corporate restructuring of Google. Alphabet serves as the parent company of Google and several other subsidiaries. Google, the most prominent subsidiary, focuses on internet-related services and products, including the search engine, advertising, and cloud computing. Alphabet's diverse portfolio also includes companies working on projects such as self-driving cars, life sciences, and urban development. The restructuring allowed Alphabet to better manage its various business ventures and investments, with a focus on innovation and technology.

Alphabet history

Alphabet Inc.'s history is a relatively short but impactful one: 2015: Alphabet Inc. is established as part of a corporate restructuring of Google. Larry Page becomes the CEO of Alphabet, and Sundar Pichai becomes the CEO of Google. 2016: Waymo, a subsidiary of Alphabet, is created to develop autonomous vehicle technology. 2018: Loon, a subsidiary focused on providing internet access via high-altitude balloons, is launched. 2019: Alphabet becomes the fourth publicly traded U.S. company to achieve a market capitalization of over $1 trillion. 2020: Alphabet-owned Google announces a quantum computer breakthrough with "quantum supremacy." Alphabet's history is characterized by its efforts to expand beyond its core search and advertising businesses into areas like autonomous vehicles, life sciences, and quantum computing, all while maintaining a strong presence in the technology industry.
