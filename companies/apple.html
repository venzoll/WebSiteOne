Apple Inc.

Official Website

Apple Inc. Founded: April 1, 1976. HQ: Cupertino, California, USA. Key Products: iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and software. Design: Known for minimalist design. Leadership: Tim Cook (as of January 2022). Impact: Valuable tech company with a focus on privacy and sustainability..

Apple is famous for quality products.

Apple history

1976: Apple is founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. 1980s: The Macintosh computer is introduced, revolutionizing personal computing. 2001: Apple releases the iPod, transforming the music industry. 2007: The iPhone is launched, changing the smartphone landscape. 2010: The iPad is introduced, creating a new category of tablet devices. 2011: Steve Jobs passes away, and Tim Cook becomes CEO. 2020s: Apple continues to innovate with products and services, focusing on privacy and sustainability.
