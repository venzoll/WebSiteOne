Amazon

Official Website

Amazon is a multinational technology and e-commerce company headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has since grown into one of the world's largest and most diversified companies. It is known for its e-commerce platform, cloud computing services (Amazon Web Services), digital streaming, artificial intelligence (Alexa), and a vast range of products and services. Amazon revolutionized online shopping and has become a major player in various industries, making it one of the most influential and successful tech companies globally.

Amazon history

Here's a brief overview of Amazon's history: 1994: Amazon is founded by Jeff Bezos in Seattle, Washington, initially as an online bookstore. 1995: The Amazon website is launched to the public, offering millions of books for sale. Late 1990s: Amazon expands into selling a wide range of products beyond books and goes public with its IPO. Early 2000s: Amazon introduces services like Amazon Prime, which offers free shipping and later includes streaming content. 2006: Amazon Web Services (AWS) is launched, becoming a leader in cloud computing services. 2010s: Amazon further diversifies into hardware with products like the Kindle e-reader, Echo smart speakers, and the acquisition of Whole Foods Market in 2017. 2020s: Amazon continues to expand its global reach and investments in technology, including AI and drone delivery. Amazon's history is a remarkable journey from an online bookstore to a global e-commerce giant with a wide array of services, products, and technological innovations.
